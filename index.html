Set up your 123 HP Printer

Download HP printer software here to set up your HP printer.
 

The 123.hp.com/setup is a part of the Hp printer device and driver setup. The setup is available for all printers, so it is known as an All-in-one printer. For setting up the printer, it is necessary to download and install the driver or software on any device. You can get the setup according to your device by entering the model number of your Hp printer device. The Hp printers are wired as well as wireless. You can use both of them. Even one can join the printer to its smartphone or Pc. For that, you will have to download the application. The all-in-one printer means the printer which provides inkjet jet, wired, wireless, copying, scanning features in it. The Hp printers can do work like, Scan, Copy, Print, and Fax.

These all-in-one Hp printers can be used at large working places because of their multifunctionality feature. The Hp laserjet pro M130fn, Hp color laserjet MFP M479dw, Hp office jet pro 8720 all in one printer, hp desk jet 3755, etc., are some examples of in one Hp printers.

The Process to arrange the 123.hp.com/setup using wifi wireless setup:-

Following are the steps or the process to arrange the Hp printer setup using wifi wireless setup;
  • The first step is to arrange the printer, i.e., connect the printer to the power switch and turn on the printer.
  • Then using the control panel of the printer, go into settings.
  • Then in settings, go into wifi networks, and in wifi, select Wireless/Ethernet networks.
  • The wifi will turn on.
  • Further, turn on the wifi device.
  • Then turn on the Laptop/Pc/Smartphone/Mac, etc.
  • On the device, click on the Start option.
  • Then search for Print in the search tab.
  • Then the 'Printer and Scanners' option will appear.
  • Click on the 'Printer and Scanners' option.
  • Turn on the wifi of the device and click on the device name to connect it.
  • Then the devices joined or connected.

The process to arrange 123.hp.com/setup Manually/wired:-

Follow the process below to arrange the Hp printer setup manually/wired;
  • The same as the above process, turn on the printer by pressing the power button.
  • Then connect one end of the USB cable to the printer.
  • And the other end to the laptop/Pc/Mac.
  • Then on the device, click on the Start option.
  • Then in the search tab, type printer and search for it.
  • Further, the 'Printer and Scanners' option will appear.
  • Then click on the 'Printer and Scanners' option.
  • In the Printer and scanners option, you will see Add device option. Click on it.
  • Then select the Hp printer device you have connected and Add it.
  • Your Hp printer will be connected manually.

Connect Hp printer to the 123.hp.com/setup driver/Software:-

  • Firstly download and install the Hp printer driver/software using website or using a CD drive.
  • Then on the other side, turn on the Hp printer.
  • Then using the control panel, enter into settings of the printer.
  • In settings, enter into wifi wireless connection.
  • Then open the device and click on the Add device option.
  • The driver/software will scan for the nearby Hp printer device.
  • Then click on Add option. The device will be added.

The steps/process to find the Hp printer device in the Windows:-

Following are the steps to find the connected Hp printer device in the windows;
  • On the desktop, you will see the Start option, which is in the Windows symbol. Click on it.
  • Then you will see the search tab.
  • There type printer and search for it.
  • Then you will see the Printer and Scanners option on the screen. Click on it.
  • Further, you will see the list of devices.
  • You will find your device name in that list.
  • If you see the name of your Hp printer device, then you are all set.
  • And if you don't see the device, then connect it again.
  • In this way, you can find your 123.hp.com/setup in windows.
